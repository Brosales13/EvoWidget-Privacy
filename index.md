# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated:** June 2026

At EvoWidget AI (“we”, “us”, or “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle technical data, analytics, and information when you use our mobile application, EvoWidget AI (the “App”).

Unlike traditional apps, we do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your real name. We believe your data belongs to you.

---

### 1. Age Restriction (18+ Only)
EvoWidget AI does not knowingly collect, process, or track data from anyone under eighteen (18) years of age. The App is strictly gated for adult use. If we discover that any automated metadata, analytics data, or device UUID activity belongs to an individual under the age of 18, we will take immediate steps to terminate the device connection and permanently purge any corresponding data from our temporary servers.

---

### 2. Information We Collect (And What We Don't)

#### A. Non-Personal Local Identification (Random UUID)
We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

* **Device Tracking:** To manage your token purchases, process video generations, and track your temporary files, the App generates a localized, random Universally Unique Identifier (UUID) on your device.
* **Anonymity:** This identifier is completely anonymous and cannot be used by us to identify you in the real world.

#### B. Usage Analytics and Performance Data
To improve your experience and ensure app stability, we collect anonymous usage data and performance analytics. 

* **App Analytics:** We track aggregated, non-personally identifiable events, such as which features are used most frequently, session duration, and overall app engagement patterns.
* **Technical Logs:** We automatically collect technical metrics, including device model, operating system version, app performance data, and crash logs.

#### C. User Inputs & Temporary Content Data
* **Inputs:** To create animated widgets, you provide text prompts or images ("Inputs"). These inputs are transmitted securely via API to our third-party AI provider (Google Gemini) solely to process and generate your request.
* **Outputs:** The resulting animated widgets or videos ("Outputs") are briefly hosted on our secure, temporary staging servers for a maximum of 48 hours. This transient window exists strictly to allow you time to download the file to your device or save it to iCloud. After 48 hours, the file is automatically and permanently purged from our infrastructure.

#### D. iCloud Data Isolation
EvoWidget AI integrates with Apple’s iCloud infrastructure to let you back up your completed animations.

* **Your Private Sandbox:** All files saved to iCloud are written directly into your private, user-controlled Apple iCloud account.
* **No Central Access:** We do not host, view, or have access to your personal iCloud storage container.

---

### 3. How We Use Your Data
We use the anonymous technical, operational, and analytics data collected strictly to run, maintain, and optimize the App:

* To validate and fulfill pay-per-generation consumable token purchases.
* To interface securely with the Google Gemini API to generate your requested animations.
* To provide essential, temporary file-hosting infrastructure (up to 48 hours) so your device can retrieve completed generations.
* To analyze anonymous usage trends, monitor crash logs, and improve overall app stability, user interface design, and feature performance.

---

### 4. Third-Party Data Processing

> 🤖 **Google Gemini API:** Our animation generations are powered by Google Gemini. When you submit a request, your technical inputs are processed by Google's developer API. According to Google’s enterprise API terms, data sent via these developer endpoints is processed to generate results and is generally not used to train global, public machine learning models. We encourage you to review Google's official privacy commitments regarding their AI developer tools.

> 📊 **Analytics Providers:** We utilize trusted, industry-standard third-party analytics and crash-reporting tools (such as Apple App Analytics or privacy-compliant telemetry services) to aggregate usage patterns and diagnose technical glitches. This data is entirely stripped of personal identifiers and cannot be linked back to your real-world identity.

> 💳 **Apple App Store:** All financial transactions, payments, and token authorizations are processed exclusively by Apple via the iOS App Store infrastructure. We never see, collect, or store your credit card information, billing details, or bank accounts.

---

### 5. Data Retention & Deletion

* **48-Hour Auto-Purge:** Any video or animation generated by the App is automatically and permanently deleted from our staging servers 48 hours after its initial creation.
* **Analytics Data:** Aggregated usage analytics and performance metrics are retained in a non-identifiable format to help us understand historical app performance and trends.
* **UUID Deletion:** Because your anonymous tracking token is a random device UUID, deleting the EvoWidget AI application from your iOS device will permanently sever and delete the connection to that identifier from your device instance.

---

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any adjustments by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document, or by presenting an in-app notice. Your continued use of EvoWidget AI after revisions go into effect constitutes your acknowledgment of the updated policy.
